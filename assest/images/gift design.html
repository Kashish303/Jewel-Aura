BlueStone Jewellery Store - A Stellar Omnichannel Presence

BlueStone, founded in 2011 by Gaurav Singh Kushwaha in Bangalore, is one of India's largest e-commerce portal for fine jewellery. By seamlessly integrating online and physical retail channels, BlueStone has transformed the way consumers experience jewellery shopping. With over 195 retail stores spread across the nation, we are committed to making exquisite fine jewellery accessible. Our omnichannel approach ensures that customers can explore BlueStone's extensive collection of fine jewellery at our online jewellery store or a retail store near them. Whether browsing through the curated selection on the website or visiting one of our retail stores, customers have access to a wide range of exquisite designs crafted with precision and attention to detail.

Redefining the Jewellery Shopping Experience

At BlueStone, we're dedicated to enhancing your jewellery shopping experience with unmatched convenience. Our Try at Home and Virtual Jewellery Consultations enable you to browse and try on our gold and diamond jewellery from the comfort of your home. Our Lifetime Exchange and Buyback policy ensures that you shop with the peace of mind that your investment lasts a lifetime. If you're bored of your outdated old gold jewellery, our Big Gold Upgrade enables you to exchange your old 18kt gold for the value of 22kt gold. To make your jewellery shopping experience even better, we offer free shipping on all online orders. And in case things don't work out as planned, we offer hassle-free 30 Day Free Returns so you can shop without a care in the world.

7000+ Certified Jewellery Designs

Our jewellery is certified by prestigious authorities such as BIS Hallmark, SGL, IGI, and GSI to ensure the authenticity and quality of every piece. Our extensive range of 7000+ contemporary creations across 100+ collections tells a unique story, each inspired by different facets of life. From gold and platinum to diamonds and gemstones, BlueStone offers 100% certified jewellery designs, promising something to suit every mood, moment, and budget. Explore our wide range of categories, which includes gold and diamond rings, earrings, pendants, mangalsutras, bangles, engagement rings, bracelets and more.
